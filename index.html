About

A personal homage recreation of the antlii.work toolset by Anatolii Babii — original implementations inspired by his generative instruments, rebuilt from scratch on p5.js, Paper.js, opentype.js and Tweakpane. Not affiliated with or endorsed by the original author.

Earlier raster experiments live in the classic tools archive.
